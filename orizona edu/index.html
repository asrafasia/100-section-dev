Welcome to the Department of Computer Science!

As you may have noticed, we have moved to a new website! Please inform us of any issues or concerns at webmaster@cs.arizona.edu. Please note that the old site is archived at http://www2.cs.arizona.edu/. If there is a page that you cannot find here on the new site, please try searching through the archived site.

The Department of Computer Science has a vibrant history of nearly forty years on campus. With a vibrant faculty and student roster, we offer a world-class environment for learning and research where your professor knows your name, you can start doing research from your sophomore year, and you'll learn cutting-edge advances from the very people who discovered them.

We are part of the UA College of Science, studying and working in the Gould-Simpson Building where the camera for the Mars Rover was built.

Our courses and our research span established areas of systems software, databases, networking, security, algorithms, and computer graphics, as well as emerging areas such as bioinformatics, cloud computing, and ergalics. Our faculty and students collaborate in interdisciplinary research across the university and across the world.

Why Study Computer Science?

Computer Science is a growing field with plenty of exciting opportunities! With a median annual salary that is more than double the national average and many interesting and challenging problems to tackle, come see why computer science is for you!
